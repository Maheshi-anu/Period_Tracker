Period Tracking System 

✅ # Features

## Cycle Prediction
- Predicts the **next period date** based on the user's cycle length and last recorded period.
- Estimates **ovulation day**, **fertile window**, and **peak pregnancy chance days**.
- Helps users better understand their menstrual cycle patterns.

## Key Cycle Insights
- Displays important reproductive cycle dates, including:
  - Next period
  - Ovulation day
  - Fertile window
  - High pregnancy probability days

## Calendar-Based Tracking
- Shows **previously recorded period dates** on an interactive calendar.
- Helps users visually track their menstrual history over time.

## Color-Coded Cycle Visualization
- Calendar highlights different cycle phases with colors:
  - **Period days**
  - **Ovulation day**
  - **Fertile window**
  - **Peak pregnancy chance days**
- Makes cycle patterns easy to understand at a glance.

## Cycle Calculation Explanation
- Explains how the app predicts cycle events based on:
  - User-entered **cycle length**
  - Estimated **ovulation timing**
  - **Fertile window calculations**

## Educational Cycle Information
- Provides clear guidance about:
  - How menstrual cycles work
  - What does ovulation means
  - When fertility is highest

## Important Health Notes
- Informs users that:
  - Menstrual cycles can vary due to stress, illness, or hormonal changes.
  - Cycle predictions are **estimates and not 100% accurate**.
  - The app works best for **people with relatively regular cycles**.

## Safety & Medical Disclaimer
- Clearly states that the app:
  - Is **for information and awareness only**
  - **Should not be used as a primary contraception method**
  - Recommends consulting healthcare professionals for medical advice.

## Usage Guidance
- Provides recommendations on how to best use the app:
  - Track cycles consistently for several months
  - Observe personal cycle patterns
  - Stay aware of natural body rhythms
